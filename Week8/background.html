About Me
Just to give you persepective on how I can help you be better at your chosen game I'll give you a little bit of what I have acheived in each
game.

CS:GO
I have spent most of my time playing CS:GO. I have played on and off for more than 5 years and am approaching the 10k hour benchamark
that most people say you have to acheive to really understand the game. I play with current and formal pros and soon-to-be pros on a regular
basis and am constantly exposed to new methods of playing the game whether it's general awareness of what is happening, map control,
utility, strats, good lurks, proper comms, etc. I have a lot of knowledge that I can give, and look forward to helping you get to your next level.

Valorant
Valorant has a lot of the same principles as does CS:GO. I spent some time playing with friends, before getting serious about the game and
grinding to Radiant. Sometimes there is a daunting level of inconsistency in lower ranks like Gold and Plat, but with the right help you
can get to play in the Immortal, and Radiant ranks. We will work on your mental fortitude, and overall understanding of the game to get you there.

Apex Legends
Apex Legends is by far one of my favorite game to grind in my off time with Counterstrike. There is just a very unique style of play about Apex.
I have played at the highest level of Diamond in Apex. I know what it takes to be good, and to be good consistently. If you struggle with the
consistency of placemenet and going positive every game, then I can help you. One incredibly underrated tip that I will give you for free is that you
MUST have fun. If the game you are grinding becomes a burden it's time to take a break, or re-think the game you play all-together.
